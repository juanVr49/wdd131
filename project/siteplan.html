Music and Productivity Site Plan

Site Name

Music and Productivity

Music is an integral part of most people's lives, and I want to learn more about its benefits for focus and productivity.

Optional domain: productivebeats.org

Purpose

The objective is to demonstrate the substantial benefits of music in a way for productivity.

I want to highlight the pros and cons and increase awareness of the importance of music in daily routines.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: What type of music helps me focus while studying?
  • Scenario 2: What music works best for relaxing after stress?

Color Schema

Primary color: #1d4e89 (Blue) used for header background, heading, and buttons.

Accent color: #e5e7eb (Light gray) used for links, highlights, and small accents.

Typography

Font family: 'Roboto', sans-serif for body text for readability.

Headings: 'Montserrat', sans-serif for headings to create a modern look.

Wireframe

Mobile Layout

  • Header (site name/logo)
  • Navigation (stacked or hamburger)
  • Hero / intro section
  • Main content sections (single column)
  • Interactive tool / form
  • Footer

Desktop Layout

  • Header (logo left, nav right)
  • Hero (wide)
  • Main content (2-column layout: content + tips/sidebar)
  • Interactive tool / form (wider layout)
  • Footer
